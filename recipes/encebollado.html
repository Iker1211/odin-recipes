Encebollado

Description

The encebollado is a soup traditional of the Coast of the Ecuador. Fish never mande in home.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of fresh tuna
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground achiote (annatto)
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 4-5 yuca (cassava) roots
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 8 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fish Broth
  2. Cook the Yuca
  3. Prepare the Base
  4. Combine Ingredients
  5. Serve
